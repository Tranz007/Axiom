Edition strategy

One core. Different trust postures.

Axiom should share one language foundation across Axiom OS, business governance, and government environments. Axiom OS proves the local contract model before heavier trust and deployment editions build on top.

Axiom OS

Local open-source core

The local CLI, language spec, examples, policy simulation, generated contracts, and runtime guards. Its job is to prove the model in the open-source core.

Axiom Business

Enterprise governance

Organization policy profiles, SSO, approval workflow integrations, audit dashboards, CI checks, evidence reports, and enterprise connectors.

Axiom Government

High-trust environments

GovCloud and restricted-environment deployment, default-deny profiles, signed bundles, review packs, attestations, and stricter audit defaults.

Why start with Axiom OS?

Enterprise and government editions should not be packaging around an unproven idea. The local CLI, examples, packaging, and docs need to feel useful first.
