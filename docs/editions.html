Enterprise & Gov direction

Governance paths for teams that need more control.

Axiom OS is the open-source foundation available now. Enterprise and Government editions are future product directions for organizations that need stronger policy management, evidence reporting, audit workflows, and deployment verification.

Axiom OS

Local open-source core

The local CLI, language spec, examples, policy simulation, generated contracts, and runtime guards. Its job is to prove the model in the open-source core.

Future direction

Enterprise governance

Planned focus areas include shared policy profiles, team governance, approval workflow integration, reporting, and operational controls.

Future direction

Government and regulated environments

Planned focus areas include stricter policy traceability, evidence reporting, deployment verification, private/internal use, and audit review support.

Why start with the open-source foundation?

Enterprise and government editions should not be packaging around an unproven idea. The local CLI, examples, contract workflow, simulations, generated artifacts, and docs need to be useful first. The future paths should add operational depth without hiding the foundation.
